# Lesson 2.8 — Week 2 Practical Lab + Teach‑Back Prep

## Outcome
You can demonstrate Week 2 competence live, without fumbling.

---

## Practical Lab (End‑to‑End)

### Scenario
A lead responds to your brand awareness email asking for help. They also want to see proof you’re active on social.

You must:
1) confirm GHL orientation competence
2) show Social Planner execution (manual + CSV)
3) show Support intake path (ticket created + assigned)
4) show Workflow replication skill (Pets workflow fixed and tested)
5) present a short “Business Understanding” teach‑back

### Required Proof Artifacts
- 1 Loom video (5–10 minutes) walking through the above.
- Screenshots:
  - Social Planner queue
  - Support pipeline ticket
  - Workflow execution proof for test contact

---

## Teach‑Back Agenda (Fri 4–5pm)
1) **Business Understanding (5 minutes):**
   - what we do
   - who we do it for
   - how GHL supports delivery
2) **Social Planner demo (10 minutes):**
   - show manual post scheduling
   - show CSV import + validation
3) **Support system demo (10 minutes):**
   - show pipeline stages
   - show intake form
   - show workflow creates ticket + assigns owner
4) **Workflow replication demo (10 minutes):**
   - show the “bad step”
   - show your fix
   - show test proof
5) Q&A / gaps (rest of time)

---

## Pass/Fail Criteria
Pass if:
- You can demo everything without confusion.
- You show proof artifacts (not just explanations).
- Your escalation path and ownership are correct.

Fail if:
- You can’t show actual tickets/posts/workflow execution.
- You changed production without duplication/testing.
